K. V. Chebotarev (Moscow) A. M. Listopadov’s expedition map

Summary: The article is devoted to the experience of creating an inter- active online map of A. M. Listopadov’s expeditions based on the data available in the five-volume “Songs of the Don Cossacks”. The development process, problems encountered during the work and ways to solve them are described.
